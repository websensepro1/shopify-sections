## What is the Shopify Knowledge Base App?
Simply put, this app makes your store intelligible to AI shopping assistants like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Perplexity. When a shopper uses these AI agents to search for products or ask about store policies, the AI needs clear, structured data to pull from.

The Shopify Knowledge Base app does two critical things:

**Provides Analytics:** It shows you what questions AI agents are asking about your store and products.
**Lets You Customize FAQs:** It allows you to provide direct answers to common questions, ensuring AI represents your brand accurately.
It’s your direct line to the new “AI search engine,” and it’s completely free.

## Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up the Shopify Knowledge Base
# Step 1: Install the App
From your Shopify admin dashboard, go to **Apps** > **Shopify App Store**.
Search for **“Shopify Knowledge Base“.**
Click on the app (it will be listed as “built by Shopify”) and press **Add app.**

# Step 2: Explore the Main Dashboard

Once installed, the app’s main dashboard is your command center. You’ll see three key sections:

**Product Query Volume**: See how often your products are being requested by AI agents.
**Top Product Requests**: A list of your most frequently asked-about products.
**FAQ Query Log**: The heart of the app. This shows you the specific questions AI agents are asking about your store policies, shipping, etc.

# Step 3: Analyze & Answer Unanswered Questions

This is the most powerful feature. The app will proactively suggest questions you should answer based on what the AI is seeing (or not seeing) on your store.

In the dashboard, scroll down to the **“Unanswered questions”** section.
You’ll see questions like:
“Do you have retail store locations?”
“Do you offer bulk or wholesale orders?”
“How can I contact customer service?”
Click the **“Answer”** button next to a question.
A pop-up will appear where you can type a clear, concise answer. For example:
**Question:** Do you have retail store locations?
**Answer:** Yes! We have a flagship store located at 123 Main Street, Anytown, USA.

# Step 4: Review Automatically Linked Sources
Shopify automatically pulls information from your existing store settings to answer some questions.

In your FAQ list, you’ll see questions like “What language is your store available in?” or “Do you offer pickup?”
The app will show a **“Source”** link (e.g., “Language,” “Pickup,” “Customer accounts”).
Clicking this source will take you directly to the relevant Shopify settings page, allowing you to update that information if needed.
